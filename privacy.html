1. INTRODUCTION

Scan & Grab ("us," "we," or "Company") is committed to respecting the privacy rights of visitors at our website at www.scan_Grab.com, and also of the registered users of the Scan & Grab application (collectively, "the Sites"). We created this Privacy Policy ("Privacy Policy") to demonstrate our commitment to fair information practices and the protection of privacy. Scan & Grab's consumer shopping technology includes software that enables its users to both scan the barcode of consumer goods, and also to pay for such goods using the Scan & Grab application ("Service"). The personal information that Scan & Grab processes, collects, and stores is for the purpose of enabling your use of the Service.

This Privacy Policy is only applicable to the Sites and the Service available thereon, and not applicable to any other websites that you may be able to access from the Sites via links, each of which may have data collection, storage, and use practices and policies that differ materially from this Privacy Policy. By using Company's Service you consent to Company's use of the information you provide in connection with creating a Scan & Grab account, as set forth in this Privacy Policy. Except as otherwise provided in this Privacy Policy, we will keep your Personal Information private and will not share it with third parties, unless such disclosure is necessary to: (a) comply with a court order or other legal process; (b) protect our rights or property; or (c) enforce our Terms of Service. Your Personal Information is stored on secure servers that are not accessible by third parties.

2.1. USER ABILITY TO ACCESS, UPDATE, AND CORRECT PERSONAL INFORMATION

We maintain a procedure in order to help you confirm that your Personal Information remains correct and up-to-date. Our Sites and corresponding Service are not intended for, or designed to attract, individuals under the age of 13.
